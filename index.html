Shape Your Life with the Words of Life.

1 Timothy 4:12 (NIV): "Dont let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity." This verse encourages young people not to underestimate their impact. Regardless of age, they are called to set a positive example in their words, actions, love, faith, and purity. It emphasizes that youth can play a significant role in influencing others and contributing to the community of believers.
